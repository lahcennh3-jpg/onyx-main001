## Connector Rules

- Do not touch unauthorized connectors.
- Do not publish connector URLs, workspace names, tenant names, access tokens, refresh tokens, or secret values.
- Confirm connector owner before testing.
- Confirm whether connector permissions are inherited from the source system or mapped inside Onyx.
- Record sync scope and document visibility assumptions.
- Use synthetic or explicitly approved data only.
